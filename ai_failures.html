9. AI "failures"

Survey Question: "Tell us about a time AI really failed you or caused unexpected trouble. What happened, and what did you learn?"

Prompting & Instruction Issues

Poor prompts lead to wrong outputs. Users need domain knowledge to judge results effectively.

Accuracy & Hallucination

AI gives confidently wrong answers, hallucinates fake references, and produces inconsistent results.

Code Generation Problems

Generates non-working code, suggests breaking changes, and outputs outdated syntax.

Context & Reasoning Issues

Forgets context, contradicts itself, struggles with long texts and multi-step reasoning.

Positive Experiences

Some users report no major failures, especially when using AI cautiously with proper expectations.

Key Insights

AI failures stem from fundamental limitations in reasoning, context understanding, and reliability. The most common issues are hallucination, poor prompting, and technical inaccuracies. Users who approach AI cautiously with proper expectations report fewer problems.
